Frequently Asked Questions

What is AI Writer?

An AI writer is a tool that uses artificial intelligence to generate written content, correct grammar, and assist with tasks like articles, emails, and social media posts automatically.

Can the app generate essays and articles on any topic?

Yes, the app uses AI technology to generate essays and articles on a wide variety of topics. You can provide specific input or keywords, and the app will create well-structured content accordingly.

Does the app have a built-in grammar and spell checker?

Yes, the app includes an advanced grammar and spell checker to ensure that your essays and articles are free from errors.

Can I customize the tone and writing style of my essays or articles?

Absolutely! The app allows you to choose between different writing styles and tones, such as formal, informal, persuasive, or creative, depending on your needs.

Is the app available in multiple languages?

Yes, the app supports multiple languages, allowing you to generate content in your preferred language, whether it’s English, Spanish, French, or others.

Can I use the app offline?

You need an internet connection to generate new content and access certain features. However, once an essay or article is generated, you can access it offline.
